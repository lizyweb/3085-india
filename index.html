India Skill Development Center

India Skill Development Center, Recognised by Ministry of MSME, NSD, NSC, & DGTE, Ministry of Labour Government of India New Delhi 110001 offers comprehensive education and training programs. We provide placements, sponsorships, and various courses, including digital marketing, business development, and coaching for medical, engineering, and public examinations.

Our center facilitates computer training, laptop sales, and courses in electronics, with a focus on women empowerment and skill development.

Providing UGC-approved education and arranging scholarships by the Government of India after one year of admission, both in India and abroad.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution in skill development and professional training, recognized for our commitment to excellence and our role in shaping the future workforce. We aspire to create an inclusive learning environment that encourages lifelong learning and continuous improvement. By collaborating with industry leaders and leveraging cutting-edge technology, we strive to equip our students with the tools they need to succeed in a rapidly evolving global landscape.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in their chosen fields. We are dedicated to providing high-quality education and training programs that promote personal and professional growth. Through our focus on innovation, inclusivity, and industry-relevant curricula, we aim to bridge the gap between education and employment, fostering a community of skilled professionals who contribute to the development of society.

India Skill Development Center (ISDC) is proudly approved by various prestigious bodies, including the Union Government and State Governments. We are actively involved in numerous social welfare projects initiated by the Prime Minister (PM) and Chief Ministers (CM), focusing on enhancing education and skill development across the nation. Our collaborations extend to significant projects supported by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), aiming to empower individuals with the necessary skills for today’s competitive job market.

In addition to government initiatives, ISDC is dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship through start-up programs and various other innovative projects. We strive to create a supportive ecosystem for budding entrepreneurs, providing them with the resources and training needed to turn their ideas into successful ventures. Our commitment to social welfare and skill development ensures that we contribute meaningfully to the community, driving economic growth and social progress.
